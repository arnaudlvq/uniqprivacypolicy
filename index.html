Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of Uniq Winders. Your privacy is important to us. This document outlines the minimal data practices followed by our app.

What We Do Not Collect

- We do not log users in.
- We do not gather any personal information about users.
- We do not track or send any data to third-party services.

What We Do Store

The only data stored on our webserver is:

  • The ID of the winder
  • The parameters, including:
    • Number of cycles
    • Cycle duration
    • Running state

All data is stored anonymously and cannot be linked back to any user.

Conclusion

Uniq Winders is committed to maintaining your privacy. If you have any questions about this policy, please feel free to contact us.
